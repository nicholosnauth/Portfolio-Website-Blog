Skills Overview

I am a skilled software developer with a strong focus on Android development, specializing in Kotlin and Jetpack Compose. I have extensive experience building high-performance, user-friendly applications for Android, leveraging the full capabilities of the Android SDK and Jetpack libraries to create modern, responsive UIs and seamless user experiences.

While my primary expertise lies in Kotlin and Android development, I also have experience in iOS development using Swift, though my main strength is in crafting native Android apps. For cross-platform development, I am proficient in React Native, which allows me to maintain high-quality, consistent apps across both Android and iOS.

I have a solid understanding of backend integrations, including RESTful APIs and Firebase for real-time data storage and cloud services. I also use SQLite for local databases to ensure smooth offline functionality within apps.

In terms of tools, I work with Android Studio, Xcode, Git, and GitHub for version control, streamlining collaboration and maintaining high standards of code quality. I am also adept at testing with tools like JUnit, Espresso, and XCTest to ensure apps are stable and bug-free.

Beyond mobile development, I have experience with web technologies such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, and SQL, allowing me to contribute to full-stack development when needed. I follow Material Design and Human Interface Guidelines (HIG) for UI/UX design, ensuring my apps are both intuitive and visually appealing.
