What are my skills?

I am a versatile software developer with a strong focus on **native mobile development**. My expertise lies in creating high-performance, user-friendly applications for both **Android** and **iOS** using **Kotlin** and **Swift**. I have built several native apps that leverage the full potential of each platform, delivering seamless and optimized user experiences.

In addition to **Kotlin** and **Swift**, I have experience with modern mobile development frameworks like **Jetpack Compose** for Android and **Xcode** for iOS, which help me craft sleek, responsive UIs that feel native to each platform. I also work with **React Native** for cross-platform app development, ensuring consistency and quality across both Android and iOS devices.

I am skilled in **Firebase** for real-time data storage, as well as integrating **RESTful APIs** for smooth backend communication. I understand the importance of testing, so I’m familiar with tools like **JUnit**, **Espresso**, and **XCTest** to ensure my apps are stable and bug-free.

Beyond mobile development, I also have experience in **web technologies** like **HTML**, **CSS**, **JavaScript**, **PHP**, and **SQL**, allowing me to build full-stack applications when needed. My work with frameworks like **React.js** and **Node.js** has given me a solid foundation for both front-end and back-end development.

I’m passionate about creating intuitive **UI/UX designs**, guided by principles like **Material Design** and **Human Interface Guidelines** (HIG). I also have a self-taught background in **graphic design**, **UX**, and **content creation**, which I developed further during my time at **Trent University**.

My portfolio showcases my ability to deliver both robust solutions and visually appealing designs, making me a valuable asset for any mobile or web development project.
